Instructions

Startup

  • Select a category (eg. Music) to practice at either intermediate or senior difficulty.
  • Select a subcategory (eg. Pop Music) and get ready to play!

Gameplay Controls

  • Press SPACE to buzz in for an answer
  • Press ENTER to submit your answer
  • Press N to move to the next question

Extra Rules

  • If your answer to a question is close but not quite right, you will be "prompted", this is a second chance to fix your answer!
About

PeakPerformance was developed by Paul Siri and Jared Cubilla, two students from Cameron Heights Collegiate Institute, over the course of two-ish weeks in July 2018. Paul and Jared are passionate members of their school's "Reach For The Top" team, a local competitive trivia club with elements of both Quiz Bowl and Jeopardy. They looked to quiz applications like QuizUp and Protobowl in hopes of improving their game, but these apps, while helpful, could have been much improved. Paul and Jared decided to develop their own trivia app, with the goal of being able to truly reach for the top; to attain PeakPerformance.
